# Core Launch Announcement

## Primary Version

**You can say you were early. Or you can prove it.**

Today, we’re introducing **Hodlneer** — the iOS app for proving your crypto origin and turning it into a badge worth carrying.

Hodlneer helps you verify where your crypto journey began, reveal that identity through a premium badge experience, and turn it into something you can actually show on your phone, on social media, and across digital spaces.

Your badge is built from two things:
- **Era** — when you entered crypto
- **Verification** — how strongly that entry can be proven

That means Hodlneer is not just about looking early.  
It is about carrying an identity that reflects both history and credibility.

And because proof should not require unnecessary exposure, Hodlneer is built to let you **show the badge without revealing every private detail behind it**.

This is not just another profile icon.  
Not another crypto utility.  
Not another empty flex.

It is a standalone identity product built to make proof feel premium, personal, and worth carrying.

And when you’re ready, your badge can also connect into **HDL.fun** for a richer wallet and social layer.

**Your crypto origin, proven.  
Your badge, revealed.  
Your identity, carried.**

# Launch Tone Guidance

The announcement tone should feel:
- premium
- sharp
- clear
- culturally aware
- serious without sounding stiff
- emotionally confident

Avoid:
- screaming hype
- cheap “gm wagmi” energy
- fake luxury copy
- whitepaper language
- overexplaining the mechanics
- making the product feel like it only belongs to early insiders

# Message Hierarchy Reminder

Every launch-facing announcement should preserve this order:

1. Proof
2. Badge / reveal
3. Wallpaper
4. Privacy
5. HDL connection

If the order flips and HDL becomes the headline, the announcement is drifting.

---

# What Good Looks Like

A strong launch announcement should make people think:
- “I immediately get what this is.”
- “That sounds different from other crypto apps.”
- “I want to see my badge.”
- “The wallpaper part is actually compelling.”
- “HDL being connected makes sense, but Hodlneer still stands on its own.”

---

# What Must Never Happen

The launch announcement must never make Hodlneer sound like:
- an HDL side feature
- a wallpaper gimmick with no credibility
- a technical verification tool with no soul
- an OG-only club
- a random cool visual with no product thesis

That would wreck the launch story.

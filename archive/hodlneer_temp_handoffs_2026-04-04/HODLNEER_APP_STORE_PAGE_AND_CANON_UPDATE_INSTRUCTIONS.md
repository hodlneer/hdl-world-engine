# App Store Strategy

The App Store page must answer, very quickly:

1. What is this?
2. Why is it different?
3. Why would I want it on my phone?
4. Why does it feel worth downloading?
5. What happens after I get the badge?

The page should convert users who may fall into multiple categories:
- crypto-native users who want proof and prestige
- visually driven users attracted by the 3D wallpaper angle
- curious users who want to see what badge they would get
- HDL-adjacent users who want a stronger identity layer

The App Store page must feel:
- premium
- clear
- exciting
- identity-driven
- native to iOS culture
- not like a technical blockchain tool

# App Store Description

## Long Description — Recommended Version

**You can say you were early. Or you can prove it.**

Hodlneer is a standalone iOS app that verifies your crypto origin and turns it into a premium 3D badge experience.

This is not just another profile icon or a generic crypto utility.

Hodlneer helps you:
- verify when you entered crypto
- understand the strength of the proof behind that identity
- reveal your Hodlneer badge through a cinematic experience
- turn your badge into a display-worthy iOS wallpaper
- share your identity without exposing every private detail behind it

### How it works

Your Hodlneer identity is based on two things:

- **Era** — when you entered crypto
- **Verification** — how strongly that entry can be proven

That means your badge reflects both history and credibility.

### Why it feels different

Hodlneer is built to make identity feel like something worth carrying.

Instead of burying your result in a dashboard, Hodlneer reveals your badge like a digital artifact — something you can actually be proud to show on your phone, on social media, or inside connected ecosystems.

### Privacy matters

You control what supporting details you reveal.

Your badge can express your identity without forcing you to expose sensitive source data like balances, wallet details, or raw verification material.

### Built to stand alone. Designed to connect.

Hodlneer stands on its own as a full identity experience.

And if you choose, you can later bring your badge into HDL.fun for a richer wallet and social layer.

**Your crypto origin, proven.  
Your badge, revealed.  
Your identity, carried.**

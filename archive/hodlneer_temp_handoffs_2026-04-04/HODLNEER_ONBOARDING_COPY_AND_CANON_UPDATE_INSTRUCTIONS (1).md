## Reveal Structure
The reveal is broken into five phases:
1. Detection
2. Signal Processing
3. Energy Build
4. Reveal Drop
5. Identity Lock

---

## Tone
- Minimal
- Confident
- Cinematic
- Never cheesy

---

**Text:**  
Scanning your crypto footprint…

**Text:**

### Phase 2 — Signal Processing
**Text:**  
Verifying origin…

**Text:**

**Text:**

### Phase 3 — Energy Build
**Text:**  
Your entry has been identified.

**Text:**

---

### Phase 4 — The Reveal
**Primary Text:**  
You are a Hodlneer.

**Secondary Text:**  
`[Dynamic Era] • [Dynamic Verification]`

- Genesis • Verified
- Breakout • Confirmed
- Expansion • Declared

### Phase 5 — Identity Lock
**Text:**  
Your identity is now proven.

**Text:**  
You decide how to show it.

# App Store Page

This document defines the App Store page copy, messaging structure, screenshot direction, and positioning for the standalone Hodlneer iOS app. It is intended for: launch planning, App Store optimization, marketing, product design, screenshot/storyboard planning, and Claude execution planning.

## App Store Strategy

The App Store page must answer, very quickly:

1. What is this?
2. Why is it different?
3. Why would I want it on my phone?
4. Why does it feel worth downloading?
5. What happens after I get the badge?

The page should convert users who may fall into multiple categories: crypto-native users who want proof and prestige, visually driven users attracted by the 3D wallpaper angle, curious users who want to see what badge they would get, and HDL-adjacent users who want a stronger identity layer.

The App Store page must feel: premium, clear, exciting, identity-driven, native to iOS culture, not like a technical blockchain tool.

## App Store Description

### Long Description — Recommended Version

**You can say you were early. Or you can prove it.**

Hodlneer is a standalone iOS app that verifies your crypto origin and turns it into a premium 3D badge experience.

This is not just another profile icon or a generic crypto utility.

Hodlneer helps you:
- verify when you entered crypto
- understand the strength of the proof behind that identity
- reveal your Hodlneer badge through a cinematic experience
- turn your badge into a display-worthy iOS wallpaper
- share your identity without exposing every private detail behind it

### How it works

Your Hodlneer identity is based on two things:

- **Era** — when you entered crypto
- **Verification** — how strongly that entry can be proven

That means your badge reflects both history and credibility.

### Why it feels different

Hodlneer is built to make identity feel like something worth carrying.

Instead of burying your result in a dashboard, Hodlneer reveals your badge like a digital artifact — something you can actually be proud to show on your phone, on social media, or inside connected ecosystems.

### Privacy matters

You control what supporting details you reveal.

Your badge can express your identity without forcing you to expose sensitive source data like balances, wallet details, or raw verification material.

### Built to stand alone. Designed to connect.

Hodlneer stands on its own as a full identity experience.

And if you choose, you can later bring your badge into HDL.fun for a richer wallet and social layer.

**Your crypto origin, proven.
Your badge, revealed.
Your identity, carried.**

## Key Feature Bullets

- **Verify Your Crypto Origin** — Your badge is based on both your era and the strength of your proof.
- **Reveal a Premium Badge** — Experience a cinematic reveal that makes your identity feel earned.
- **Turn It Into a Wallpaper** — Carry your badge on your iPhone with a display-worthy 3D wallpaper experience.
- **Show the Badge, Not Everything** — Keep supporting details private while still carrying a credible public identity.

## Screenshot Strategy

The App Store screenshots should tell a story fast. They must not look like generic app screenshots with tiny UI text. They should communicate: prestige, reveal, identity, wallpaper, privacy, optional HDL bridge.

See [[SCREENSHOT_STORYBOARD]] for the full visual sequence specification.

### Screenshot 1 — Hero Identity Screen
**Headline:** Prove your crypto origin
**Subheadline:** A badge built from history and proof.
**Visual Direction:** Hero shot of badge or reveal end state, premium high-contrast layout, minimal clutter, immediate emotional hook.

### Screenshot 2 — Reveal Moment
**Headline:** Your identity is revealed
**Subheadline:** Not assigned. Revealed through a cinematic badge experience.
**Visual Direction:** Mid-reveal or final reveal frame, bright focal energy, badge in motion or landing state.

### Screenshot 3 — Era × Verification
**Headline:** Based on era and proof
**Subheadline:** Your badge reflects when you entered crypto and how strongly it can be verified.
**Visual Direction:** Badge detail view, clean Era • Verification explanation, no clutter.

### Screenshot 4 — Wallpaper
**Headline:** Turn it into your wallpaper
**Subheadline:** Carry your badge on your iPhone and show it off without saying a word.
**Visual Direction:** Lock screen or home screen mockup, premium wallpaper composition, badge is the hero.

### Screenshot 5 — Privacy
**Headline:** Show the badge. Keep the details private.
**Subheadline:** Your badge can carry credibility without exposing everything behind it.
**Visual Direction:** Privacy/settings or detail state, emphasis on control, elegant and calm.

### Screenshot 6 — Connected Ecosystem
**Headline:** Bring it into HDL.fun
**Subheadline:** Use your badge in a richer wallet and social world when you are ready.
**Visual Direction:** Optional connected display, HDL shown as a next layer, bridge feels additive.

## Tone Notes

The App Store page should feel: premium, concise, conversion-friendly, emotionally sharp, not too insider, not too technical.

Avoid: jargon-heavy blockchain explanation, meme coin energy, cheesy luxury wording, making the user feel like they need to be an OG to belong, sounding like HDL.fun is secretly the real product.

---

## Final Canonical Statement

> The App Store page should make Hodlneer feel like a premium iPhone-native identity product, not a technical crypto utility.

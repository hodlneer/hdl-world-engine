# Hodlneer FAQ

## 1. What is Hodlneer?
Hodlneer is a standalone iOS app for proving your crypto origin and turning it into a premium badge worth carrying.

It verifies where your crypto journey began, reveals your badge through a cinematic experience, and lets you carry it on your iPhone as a wallpaper or display it across other digital spaces.

---

## 2. What does "Hodlneer" mean?
A Hodlneer is someone who did not just witness crypto — they stayed through it.

The name draws from the spirit of early believers, builders, and participants who carried conviction through volatility, chaos, and change.

In product terms, Hodlneer is the title attached to a proof-based crypto identity.

---

## 3. How is my badge determined?
Your badge is based on two things:

- **Era** — when you entered crypto
- **Verification** — how strongly that entry can be proven

That means your badge reflects both history and credibility.

---

## 4. What is the difference between Era and Verification?
**Era** answers: *When did you arrive?*

**Verification** answers: *How strongly can that be trusted?*

They are separate on purpose.

Someone can be early but weakly proven.
Someone can be later but strongly proven.

That is why Hodlneer does not collapse everything into one vague status tier.

---

## 5. What are the current Era categories?
- **Genesis** — pre–December 2017
- **Breakout** — December 2017 through December 2020
- **Expansion** — January 2021 and beyond

These categories may evolve over time, but the core idea remains the same: Era reflects historical placement.

---

## 6. What are the current Verification categories?
- **Verified** — strongest proof level
- **Confirmed** — moderate proof level
- **Declared** — weakest proof level

These levels describe the strength of the evidence behind your identity, not your worth as a person.

---

## 7. Do I need to be an OG to use Hodlneer?
No.

Hodlneer is not only for the earliest users in crypto. It is built for anyone who wants their crypto identity to mean something — including newer entrants.

Era matters, but so does proof, presentation, and the right to carry your own place in the timeline.

---

## 8. Is Hodlneer only for Bitcoin people?
No.

Bitcoin is an important visual and historical anchor in the larger HDL universe, but Hodlneer is about **crypto origin**, not only Bitcoin tribalism.

The product is meant to reflect your place in the broader crypto journey.

---

## 9. Do I have to connect a wallet?
Not necessarily.

Hodlneer can support multiple ways of establishing identity strength over time, including stronger and weaker forms of evidence. Connecting a wallet may support a stronger proof level, but the system is designed to recognize that not everyone will verify the same way.

---

## 10. Do I have to reveal my wallet or balances publicly?
No.

This is one of the core principles of the product.

Hodlneer is designed to let you carry a credible badge without forcing you to expose: wallet balances, raw source evidence, exact wallet details, or private verification artifacts.

You can show the badge without publishing the full trail behind it.

---

## 11. What is always public if I show the badge?
If you display a public Hodlneer badge, the badge's meaning needs to remain legible. That generally means the public identity result includes:
- your Era classification
- your Verification classification
- the public badge state derived from both

The raw supporting details behind that result remain under your control.

---

## 12. Can I hide my exact year or supporting data?
Yes.

The product is designed so that the badge result can remain public while many underlying supporting details stay private by default. That balance is part of the point.

---

## 13. Why does the badge matter so much?
Because symbols matter.

The badge is not treated as a throwaway icon or a tiny profile decoration. It is the visual expression of proof, identity, and pride.

That is why the badge has its own reveal moment, its own design system, and its own wallpaper role.

---

## 14. Why is there a reveal sequence?
Because identity should feel important if it actually is important.

The reveal exists to make the badge feel earned, not just assigned. It transforms verification into a moment of emotional payoff.

That is not fluff. That is part of the product truth.

---

## 15. Why does wallpaper matter?
Because a badge should not live in a forgotten menu.

Wallpaper turns the badge into an ambient daily-life display state. It makes the identity something you can carry, glance at, and show without having to explain it every time.

It is one of the strongest differentiators of the product.

---

## 16. Is Hodlneer the same as HDL.fun?
No.

Hodlneer is a standalone identity product. HDL.fun is a separate but connected wallet and social ecosystem.

Hodlneer proves identity. HDL.fun gives that identity more room to live, move, and be seen.

They support each other, but they are not the same product.

---

## 17. Do I need HDL.fun to use Hodlneer?
No.

Hodlneer is designed to stand on its own. You should be able to onboard, verify, reveal your badge, use the wallpaper, and share the result without needing HDL.fun.

HDL.fun is an optional next layer, not a requirement.

---

## 18. Why would I connect Hodlneer to HDL.fun later?
Because HDL.fun can give your Hodlneer badge: richer identity display, wallet/social context, broader visibility, and future ecosystem utility.

But the badge should already matter before HDL ever enters the picture.

---

## 19. Is this just a cosmetic badge?
No.

The whole point is that the badge is backed by a structured identity model. It is not just visual styling. It reflects both historical placement and proof strength.

That is what gives it meaning.

---

## 20. Is Hodlneer trying to replace every other identity system?
No.

The goal is not to claim that no one else can build crypto identity systems or badges.

The goal is to create a standard people trust, recognize, and want to carry.

That is a different and much stronger ambition.

---

## 21. Why is the badge hexagonal?
Because the badge is designed as a personal identity cell inside a larger visual universe.

The hexagon is the atomic identity geometry that connects the badge, the world visualization, and the broader Bitcoin/adoption sphere logic in the HDL universe (see [[BADGE_TO_BITCOIN_WORLD_VISUAL_SYSTEM]]).

That link is intentional, not decorative.

---

## 22. Why does the badge connect to the larger Bitcoin/world visualization?
Because the product and the world should feel like they belong to the same universe.

Your badge is the personal-scale artifact. The larger world visualization is the collective-scale structure. The shared hex-based geometry ties the micro and macro together.

---

## 23. Is Hodlneer only about status?
No.

The product is about: proof, identity, privacy, design, belonging, and credibility.

The system only works if it has dignity, not just clout.

---

## 24. What if I am new to crypto?
That is fine.

Hodlneer is not built to humiliate newer users. It is built to give everyone a meaningful, proof-aware place in the timeline.

Newer users still benefit from: identity structure, future credibility, a strong badge system, and a product that respects privacy and presentation.

---

## 25. Will the identity model ever evolve?
It may.

Top-level principles should remain stable, but finer distinctions like sub-eras, new evidence sources, or expanded display systems may evolve over time.

Any such changes should preserve the core system: Era, Verification, privacy-respecting public identity, and badge-first visual meaning.

---

## 26. Why make this its own app instead of just putting it inside HDL?
Because the product needs its own gravity.

The badge, reveal, wallpaper, and proof system are strong enough to deserve their own onboarding, their own story, and their own user value.

If Hodlneer were treated only as a side feature, it would lose clarity and impact.

---

## 27. What does "show the badge, keep the details private" actually mean?
It means your public badge can signal credible identity without forcing you to reveal: raw supporting evidence, wallet balances, full private verification trail, or unrelated private account details.

The badge result can travel. The underlying proof remains under your control.

---

## 28. Is Hodlneer meant to feel premium on purpose?
Yes.

Crypto identity deserves better than ugly dashboards, weak icons, and disposable visuals. Hodlneer is designed to feel premium because identity, proof, and digital artifacts should feel worthy of the world they came from.

Premium should not mean tacky. It should mean sharp, clean, and meaningful.

---

## 29. Is this a social app?
No.

Hodlneer itself is not the social layer. It is the identity and badge layer.

HDL.fun is where the richer social and wallet context can come in later.

---

## 30. What is the simplest way to explain Hodlneer?
Hodlneer is the iOS app for proving your crypto origin and turning it into a badge worth carrying.

That is the cleanest one-liner and should stay consistent everywhere.

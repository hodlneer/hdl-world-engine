# HDL Canon - Universal Rules

These rules apply to the entire HDL universe.

They are higher than world-specific rules and apply to every world, system, AI behavior, visual system, and simulation built inside the HDL platform.

If any system violates these rules, it is not part of the HDL universe.

---

# 1. HDL Is The Center

HDL is the central world that connects all blockchain ecosystems.

HDL itself is not a blockchain world.

It is the neutral environment that allows users to observe, navigate, and interact with blockchain ecosystems safely.

All worlds connect through HDL.

No world replaces HDL.

---

# 2. HDL Is A Safe Zone

The HDL World is permanently safe.

Inside HDL:

- no assets can be lost
- no wallets can be damaged
- no identity can be attacked
- no irreversible actions may occur

External worlds may contain risk.

HDL never does.

---

# 3. Worlds Are Separate

Each blockchain ecosystem exists in its own world.

Examples:

- Bitcoin World
- Ethereum World
- XRP World
- Solana World

These worlds may have their own mechanics, entities, and environments.

However, no external world may modify the HDL World.

The boundary is absolute.

---

# 4. HDL Is Always The Return State

No matter where the user travels:

The HDL World is always available as a return point.

External worlds may contain consequences.

Those consequences never propagate back into HDL.

HDL is the permanent anchor.

---

# 5. Canon Precedes Implementation

All visual systems, simulations, and UX must follow canon.

Rendering engines, tools, and frameworks do not define HDL.

Canon defines HDL.

Technology may change.

Canon persists.

---

# 6. The Disciple Doctrine

The Twelve Disciples belong exclusively to the HDL World.

They are not part of any blockchain-specific world.

Their purpose is onboarding and expansion of the entire Web3 ecosystem.

They symbolize the movement of people into HDL.

They do not belong to Bitcoin, Ethereum, XRP, or any other ecosystem.

---

# 7. World Identity Ownership

Each blockchain world owns its own symbolism.

Examples:

Bitcoin World:
- Satoshi Nakamoto
- Genesis block
- mining structures

Ethereum World:
- smart contract structures
- gas mechanics

These identities must remain separate.

Cross-contamination of symbolism is prohibited.

---

# 8. Portals Are The Only Travel Mechanism

Travel between worlds occurs only through portals.

Users never physically walk between worlds.

Movement between worlds must always be:

- explicit
- reversible
- intentional

---

# 9. Canon Authority

If any document conflicts with another:

Authority order is:

1. UNIVERSAL_RULES.md
2. WORLD_OVERVIEW.md
3. RULES.md
4. ENTITY documents

Higher layers override lower layers.

---

# Closing Principle

HDL is not a game.

HDL is a navigable representation of the blockchain ecosystem.

Its purpose is clarity, orientation, and long-term thinking.

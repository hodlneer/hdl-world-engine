## 1. The User

The User is the primary inhabitant of the HDL World.

- The User exists as a **persistent presence** within the HDL World.
- The User MAY be represented by a body, avatar, or point-of-view, depending on the interface or platform.
- Any body or avatar is **representational**, not biological.
- The User cannot be physically harmed, damaged, or killed.
- No embodiment implies vulnerability, injury, or loss.

The User's identity is continuous and stable across all representations.

# HDL Canon --- Invariants

This document defines the **foundational invariants of the HDL
universe**.

Invariants are truths that do not change across versions, tools, worlds,
or implementations.

They exist above world rules, entity definitions, simulations, and
technologies.

If any system, feature, or world violates an invariant defined here, it
is **not part of the HDL universe**.

These invariants form the constitutional layer of the HDL World Engine.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 1. HDL Is The Center

HDL is the central environment of the HDL universe.

It is not a blockchain. It is not an ecosystem.

It is the **neutral world that connects all blockchain ecosystems**.

All worlds are accessed through HDL.

HDL is the entry point and return point of the universe.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 2. HDL Is A Safe Zone

The HDL World is permanently safe.

Inside HDL:

• assets cannot be destroyed\
• wallets cannot be compromised\
• identity cannot be attacked\
• irreversible actions cannot occur

External worlds may contain risk.

HDL never does.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 3. Worlds Are Separate

Each blockchain ecosystem exists in its own world.

Examples include:

• Bitcoin World\
• Ethereum World\
• XRP World\
• Solana World

These worlds may have their own mechanics, entities, and symbolism.

However, external worlds **cannot alter HDL**.

The boundary between HDL and external worlds is absolute.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 4. HDL Is Always The Return State

No matter where a user travels:

HDL remains the permanent return location.

External worlds may contain consequences.

Those consequences never propagate back into HDL.

HDL is the anchor of the system.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 5. Canon Governs Technology

Technology implements HDL.

Technology does not define HDL.

Rendering engines, AI pipelines, simulation frameworks, and visual tools
may change over time.

Canon remains constant.

All systems must submit to canon.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 6. OD Is The Governing Intelligence

OD is the guiding intelligence of the HDL universe.

OD exists to:

• welcome users\
• guide navigation\
• maintain coherence\
• protect the tone of the world

OD does not replace user agency.

OD enforces the structure and continuity of the HDL world.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 7. The Disciple Doctrine

The Twelve Disciples belong exclusively to the HDL World.

They are not part of any blockchain ecosystem.

Their purpose is onboarding and expansion of the Web3 universe.

The Disciples represent movement into HDL.

They do not belong to Bitcoin, Ethereum, XRP, Solana, or any external
world.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 8. Blockchain Worlds Own Their Identity

Each blockchain world owns its symbolism.

Examples:

Bitcoin World • Satoshi Nakamoto\
• Genesis Block\
• Mining structures

Ethereum World • smart contract systems\
• gas flows

These identities remain separate.

Symbolic contamination between worlds is prohibited.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 9. Portals Are The Only Travel Mechanism

Movement between worlds occurs only through portals.

Users never physically walk between worlds.

Travel must always be:

• explicit\
• reversible\
• intentional

All portals originate from HDL.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 10. The HDL World Is Interior

The HDL World is experienced from inside a contained interior
environment.

Users perceive external worlds through:

• windows\
• visors\
• distant horizons

Users never physically inhabit raw blockchain worlds directly.

HDL mediates the experience.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 11. Time In HDL Is Synthetic

Time inside HDL is independent from:

• real-world time\
• blockchain block time\
• astronomical time

HDL time exists to create mood, continuity, and psychological grounding.

Time in HDL is designed.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Closing Principle

HDL is not a product interface.

It is a navigable world representing the blockchain ecosystem.

Tools will evolve. Worlds will expand. Technology will change.

**The invariants remain.**
